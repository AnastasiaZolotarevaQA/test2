A little about the site!

Each person needs his own four-legged friend similar
to him in temperament, character and activity. I think you often
noticed how pets look like their owners and vice versa, this is not
surprising, because each of us has our own rhythm of life and our own habits,
which are reflected in our pets

This site will help you learn about a variety of breeds that you may not even
have heard of. Read on the features of the care and maintenance of the future pet, find
out his character and temperament. Also study the history of the selected breed.
I hope you find this site useful and wish you the best of luck in finding the companion of
your dreams!
